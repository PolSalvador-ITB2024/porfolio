My Education

My academic background has been fundamental for the development of my technical and personal skills. From my beginnings in secondary education to the path I am currently following in the field of cybersecurity, each stage has been key to achieving my professional goals.

Compulsory Secondary Education (ESO)

School: Instituto Ramon Casas y Carbo / Anglo Mexicano Bilingual

Location: Palau Solita / Panama

Year of Completion: 2021

Description: I completed my Compulsory Secondary Education (ESO) at Instituto Ramon Casas y Carbo. I only attended for the final year; the first three years were completed in Panama at a bilingual school, where I built a solid foundation in subjects such as science, mathematics, English, and more. This stage helped me develop essential life skills.

Intermediate Vocational Training in Mechanization

School: Institut Manolo Hugué

Location: Caldes de Montbui

Year of Completion: 2024

Description: During this intermediate vocational training, I specialized in mechanization, learning about design, CNC machine programming, and operating industrial machines. This allowed me to gain a technical and applied perspective in the industrial sector and helped me realize that this was not the path I wanted for my future.

Higher Degree in Cybersecurity (Currently Enrolled)

School: ITB (Institut Tecnologic de Barcelona)

Year Started: 2024

Description: I am currently pursuing a higher degree in cybersecurity at ITB, where I focus on the protection of systems, networks, and data. My focus is on information security, learning how to identify and mitigate risks in digital environments.
